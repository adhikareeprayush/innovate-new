Through programs like the Innovation Practicum - Sprints, ProtoSem, and LaunchSem, students gain transdisciplinary skills, industry mentorship, and hands-on experience, transforming ideas into impactful, scalable innovations that strengthen Tamil Nadu’s talent pipeline and innovation ecosystem.

Innovation Practicum for
Technical Education

The Innovation Practicum is Forge’s university-accredited, curriculum-integrated, industry-aligned framework for driving transdisciplinary innovation through multidisciplinary student teams. Embedded within academic institutions, it enables students to co-create technology solutions in collaboration with industries, public sector enterprises, and tech startups. By combining structured sprints, industry challenges, and hands-on prototyping, the Practicum transforms classrooms into innovation hubs. Participants learn to move from problem identification to market-ready solutions, gaining technical depth, design thinking capability, and entrepreneurial mindset. The result is a continuous pipeline of innovation-capable engineering talent, ready to address real-world challenges while strengthening Tamil Nadu’s position as a leader in technology-driven industrial transformation.

pre-incubation

LaunchSem

LaunchSem is a pre-incubation program that supports final-year students in advancing their ProtoSem projects into fully deployable products. Participants refine prototypes through design optimisation, embedded systems integration, and manufacturing readiness. The program includes iterative testing, compliance checks, and performance validation in real-world conditions. Teams develop complete technical documentation, bill of materials (BOM), and assembly workflows. Guided by domain experts, they integrate hardware, software, and control systems for operational reliability. LaunchSem also provides access to advanced prototyping labs for final build iterations, ensuring products are production-ready and scalable for small-batch manufacturing or field deployment in industrial and applied environments.

Industrial Production
Workforce Development

Talent Enrichment In/for Industry

InnovateTN, powered by Forge’s practicum model, delivers targeted programs to build a future-ready industrial workforce. The Manufacturing Technology Practicum (MTP) serves both future and existing workforce segments, developing applied expertise in advanced digital production, intelligent manufacturing, and automation systems through real-world, hands-on projects. The Innovation Practicum for Industrial Workforce (IP-IW) focuses exclusively on existing workforce members, equipping them with innovation frameworks and technology management skills to address operational challenges and implement solutions on the shop floor. Together, these programs bridge theoretical knowledge with on-ground application, enabling industries to sustain continuous innovation and leadership in smart manufacturing.

Manufacturing Technology Practicum

The Manufacturing Technology Practicum equips engineering and polytechnic students, as well as the industrial workforce, with advanced competencies in Advanced Digital Production & Intelligent Manufacturing (ADPIM). Leveraging Forge’s proven pedagogical frameworks, expert-led delivery, and state-of-the-art facilities—spanning additive manufacturing, CNC machining, industrial automation, and digital twins—participants gain hands-on experience in solving real manufacturing challenges. Through structured modules and industry-anchored projects, learners acquire capabilities in systems thinking, rapid prototyping, automation, and data-driven process optimisation. The practicum bridges academic learning and industrial practice, producing future-ready talent that can drive operational excellence, lead digital transformation, and accelerate smart manufacturing adoption in the automotive and allied sectors.

innovation practicum

industrial workforce

The Innovation Practicum for Industrial Workforce (IP-IW) transforms skilled workers—from operators to executives—into innovation-capable professionals. Designed for manufacturing and industrial enterprises, IP-IW blends hands-on learning with technology management practices, enabling participants to solve operational challenges, improve processes, and accelerate product development. By fostering an innovation mindset across all levels, the program builds in-house capability for continuous improvement and technology adoption. Partner industries benefit from a workforce trained to integrate emerging technologies into production, streamline operations, and contribute directly to competitiveness and growth. IP-IW ensures that industry talent evolves alongside the demands of global markets and advanced manufacturing ecosystems.
